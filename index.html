Bio. Hi! I am Hai ( [,etʃ e 'aɪ] ), the incoming visiting research assistant at ISI@USC. I obtained my bachelor degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE) of Beihang University (BUAA) in July 2017.

Research Interest: My research interests mainly lie in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. Currently, I have much interest on

  • (a) sequence-to-sequence learning on structured encoding and decoding;
  • (b) semi-supervised learning on pseudo-data denoising.
